Seared Filet Mignon Steaks with Rosemary & Garlic Butter

Crisp, browned, flavorful outside with a moist and tender middle.

The perfect recipe and butter for filet mignon or your favorite cut of steak. This recipe turns out amazing every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 filet mignon steaks
  • 5-6 tablespoons rosemary & garlic flava butter (www.culinaryfusionarts.com)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon fresh cracked blacked pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

    • Steps

    1. Pat steaks dry with a papertowel to get a good sear. Season liberally with salt & pepper. Heat oil in the skillet over medium-hight heat, until it begins to shimmer. Add the steaks to the skillet to sear one side. Place 1 tablespoon of butter on top of each steak to melt. After 7-8 minutes, turn the steaks over to sear the other side.
    2. Add 2 tablespoons of the rosemary & garlic flava butter to the skillet. Using a large spoon, tip the skillet and baste the steaks with the butter. When done, remove the steaks from the skillet and transfer to a cutting board to rest for 5 minutes. Place the remaining butter on top of the steaks. Slice and serve.
